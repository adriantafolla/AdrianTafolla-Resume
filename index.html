Adrian Tafolla

Psychology

Adrian Tafolla is creative, independent, a multi-tasker, a communicator, reliable, ethical, and an overachiever. These characteristics allowed him to open his own practice of therapy and gather a lot of patients over the years of his work. Enjoys helping patients improve motor and cognitive skills. Adrian is enthusiastic and a team player. Motivates his patients throughout their treatment to see positive results. Experienced in working with children ages 10-17 with addressing a variety of different issues and overcoming each patient's obstacles. Adrian strives to help people in the best way he can. His goal ultimately is to provide the patient with a good and effective treatment. At the end of the day, Adrian does what is best for the patient and for the patient to show progress over the treatment they are placed in.

Adrian’s early work experience includes culinary arts catering, peer counseling at El Rancho High school, and customer service at Walmart Supercenter in Santa Fe Springs. During culinary arts catering he developed communication skills and time management. He was responsible for finishing meals at a certain time and providing the best customer service. For peer counseling at El Rancho High School, he developed different skills such as critical thinking, communication, and assistance of any kind. Adrian helped students and parents fill out applications such as college and financial aid applications. He helped parents and staff understand the process of applying to schools. He was responsible for presentations and college fairs for students and parents. Lastly, customer service at Walmart Supercenter is where he gathered the most experience. He was responsible for customer assistance with money orders, bill payments, as well as returns and exchanges.

Adrian’s educational background includes El Rancho High School diploma and a Bachelor's Degree at the University of California Riverside. His Bachelor's Degree major is Psychology, emphasizing in Law and Society.
